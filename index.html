About Me

Who I am?

Hi, My name is Camille. I am 21 years old, born and raised in Marietta, GA. I am a junior at the University of Georgia majoring in Psychology and minoring in Public Health. I am one in a family of eight, three of those being animals. My house is very rarely quiet especially when all three kids are home. Maybe it’s because we are Italian; everybody knows Italians are loud. It could be because we are passionate like our French ancestors. Or maybe it’s a combination of the two that make for a household of constant sound. Whatever it is, you are guarunteed to be entertained when you enter our house.

My Interest

From an early age, I have always been drawn to animals. It is both a blessing and a curse. I used to want to be a vet but I quickly learned that I could not handle it with all my emotions. Now, I am fascinated with Psychology. I love to learn about human behavior and mental processes. Some day, I hope, I will be working with people hands-on. But, as for now, I am just enjoying my journey in college, open to the many fields of work that Psychology has to offer. Aside from school, I am a member of the Alpha Omicron Pi sorority. I enjoy participating in the many philanthropy events that AOII has offered me. I love volunteering in the community and giving back where ever I can. I am a huge fan of live music and luckily I live in the most perfect city for that. Lastly, I live for traveling. I love exploring the world and the people across it. If I could, I would drop everything and travel. Last summer, I studied abroad and got the chance to visit many countries that I had always wanted. However, my goal is to visit them all one day.

After graduation, I plan to continue on with Graduate school in hopes of getting my PhD in either Clinical Psychology or Developmental Psychology. I know this sounds cliche but whatever I end up doing I hope I can make an impact on the world, big or small.
